Description of the problem

  • Our client want to start a Venezuelan fast food restaurant in Toronto, Canada.
  • It will be a restaurant specialized in "arepas" that is a food of the traditional gastronomy of Venezuela and its neighboring country Colombia.
  • Arepas are are made based on ground corn flour. They are circular and flattened and once cooked, on a metal plate, filled with meat, chicken, cheese, ham, pork and many other things.
  • Venezuelan arepas restaurants have proliferated in recent years in South America and now also in Europe, the United States and Canada.
  • Our client want to know if Toronto would be a good place to start their arepas restaurant and what would be a good location for his business in that city.

Description and use of Data

Conclusions

In principle the project is feasible and it is expected that it will have enough Venezuelan and Latin American potential clients in Toronto, to start according to the official census of Canada.
